2.1: Steps to cooking spaghetti

Down here is a list of ingredients you'll need.

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Hands
  • A can of omato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Cheese too, if you want. Unless you're allergic. ',:'[

2.2: Making the spaghetti

  1. Boil a pot of water, add some salt into it too.
  2. Once the water boiled, add the spaghetti and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  3. When it's done, strain it and let it rest in room temperature.

2.3: Making the sauce

  1. Add oil to your pan, and then add the tomato sauce.
  2. Chop the garlic and dump them into the sauce as well.
  3. Set your stove on low heat and let it cook for about 25 minutes, with some stirs in between.
  4. Once you're done, you can add them to your spaghetti and there you go!
